Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2024

1. Introduction

At Aiditor, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered multimedia editing service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Media files you upload for processing
  • Communication preferences
  • Payment information (for paid plans)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Process your media editing requests
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Improve our Service and develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Export your data

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service, enhance your user experience, and hold certain information. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. Below we describe the types of cookies we use and why:

a. Our Cookies (First-Party Cookies)

We use cookies that are essential for the operation of our Service. These include:

  • Authentication Cookies: We use access tokens and refresh tokens to identify you and keep you logged in. These are necessary for providing you with a secure and personalized experience.

b. Third-Party Cookies and Technologies

We also utilize third-party services that may set cookies or use other tracking technologies. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information:

  • PostHog: We use PostHog for analytics to understand how users interact with our Service. PostHog may use cookies to collect information about your usage patterns and help us improve our Service. For more information, please see PostHog's Privacy Policy.
  • Paddle: We use Paddle for payment processing. Paddle may use cookies to remember who you are and to enable payments without you having to re-enter your payment details. For more information, please see Paddle's Cookie Policy.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for security and performance optimization. Cloudflare may place cookies on your device to identify trusted web traffic and to improve the performance and security of our website. For details, refer to Cloudflare's Cookie Policy.

c. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to delete certain cookies. Generally, you should also be able to manage similar technologies in the same way that you manage cookies – using your browsers' preferences. Please note that if you choose to block cookies, this may impair or prevent due functioning of the Service.
  • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can manage your preferences through this banner.

If you disable cookies, some features of our Service may not operate as intended.

8. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service, provide the Service on our behalf, or assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@aiditor.ai.
